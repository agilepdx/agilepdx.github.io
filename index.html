AgilePDX Vision:

We see Portland as a world-class leader in software development and the broader creative economy. By "World Class" we mean profitable, sustainable, and joyful.

AgilePDX Mission:

Create a vibrant and successful Agile community of practice in the greater Portland metropolitan area by sharing real-world experience, distilled wisdom, and innovative ideas for Agile done well.

Recurring Meetings

In addition to our website, social media contributions and sponsorship of local events, we host three monthly meetings:
Downtown Pub Lunch - 12:00 noon to 1:30 PM on the first Friday of the month.
Westside Breakfast - 7:30 AM to 9:00 AM on the second Tuesday of the month.
Downtown Evening - 6-30 PM to 8:30 PM on the third Wednesday of the month.
You can find all the details on calagator.org, the technology event calendar.
