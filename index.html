AgilePDX Vision:

We see Portland as a world-class leader in software development and the broader creative economy. By "World Class" we mean profitable, sustainable, and joyful.

Inclusivity:

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all, regardless of level of experience, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or other similar characteristic.

AgilePDX Mission:

Create a vibrant and successful Agile community of practice in the greater Portland metropolitan area by sharing real-world experience, distilled wisdom, and innovative ideas for Agile done well.

Recurring Meetings

In addition to our website, social media contributions and sponsorship of local events, we host two monthly meetings:
Downtown Pub Lunch - 12:00 noon to 1:00 PM on the first Friday of the month.
Downtown Evening - Third Wednesday of the month: Doors open at 6:30 PM, main event starts at 7 PM and usually runs to 8 PM, we're out of the building by 8:30 PM.
Westside Cafe Lunch - 12:00 noon to 1:00 PM on the fourth Friday of the month.
You can find all the details on calagator.org, the technology event calendar.
